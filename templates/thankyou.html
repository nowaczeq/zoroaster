The idea for Zoroaster was inspired by a mobile application called Cove, developed by Humane Engineering. You can find the details of the apphere The app is only available in the United Kingdom: if you're there, check it out in the App Store!

The technical side of the project was inspired by Problem Set 9 ("Finance") of the CS50 course available at Harvard University. The course is available to everyone for free here

The music used for the notes are single notes from Ableton 13, accessed using a free 30-day trial. The names for the notes are the same ones used in Zoroaster.

The symbols of the notes, as well as the logo, have been taken from the SVG Repo. You can find it here

A huge thank you especially to CS50's Duck Debugger, without whom this project would be impossible! :)
